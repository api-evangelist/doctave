<h2 id="component-system">Component System</h2>

<p>One of the things we kept hearing from customers is authors wanting to go beyond basic Markdown features. Beyond just prose, writers also want to be able to create compelling landing pages, galleries, and call-to-actions.</p>

<p>After lots of iteration and unsatisfactory prototypes, and are excited to reveal our brand new component system! It allows you to:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Use a library of pre-built, composable UI components</li>
  <li>Easily build your own reusable components from our building blocks</li>
  <li>Drop down to custom HTML/CSS when needed</li>
</ul>

<p>We’ve taken inspiration from many different template and component systems such as <a href="https://mdxjs.com/">MDX</a>, <a href="https://markdoc.dev/">Markdoc</a>, and <a href="https://www.radix-ui.com/">Radix</a>, and created a system specifically designed for documentation and Markdown.</p>
